# Health Plan

Priorities and action items for optimizing health, vitality, energy, and mental clarity.

## Scoring Matrix

Goals scored 1-10 on three criteria:
- **Value** (10 = high value)
- **Ease/Cost** (10 = easy/low cost)
- **Risk of Success** (10 = low risk)

Total = sum of three scores (max 30)
